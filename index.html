about me

hey! my name is sylvester and i am an aspiring software engineer! i studied computer science at the University of California, Irvine, and i have fostered an interest in full-stack software development, human-computer interaction, and artificial intelligence. when i was younger, i was driven to explore technology by content creators such as MKBHD and watching Apple keynotes. however, after having an initial exposure to computer programming and code in high school, i became determined to not only become an expert in new tech, but use the knowledge i have attained to help create newer technologies and advance the ones that already exist.

i try to find a life outside of my work. catch me (attempt to) follow an exercise routine at the gym, watch (more like binge) YouTube and Netflix, and hanging (more like planning) with my fellow organizers from HackUCI.

feel free to contact me via email or connect with me on LinkedIn!
